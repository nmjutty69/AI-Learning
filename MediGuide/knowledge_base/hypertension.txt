Hypertension (high blood pressure) is a condition where the force of blood against artery walls is too high. 
It increases risk of heart disease and stroke.

Key points:
- Regular blood pressure monitoring is recommended.
- Lifestyle management includes reducing salt intake, maintaining healthy weight, and exercising.
- Common medications include ACE inhibitors, beta-blockers, and diuretics.

Red flags:
- Severe headaches, chest pain, vision problems.
- Sudden dizziness or shortness of breath.

Consult a healthcare provider for proper diagnosis and treatment.
