Diabetes is a condition where the body cannot properly regulate blood sugar levels. 
Type 2 Diabetes is common in adults and is often associated with obesity and lack of physical activity. 

Key points:
- Regular monitoring of blood glucose is essential.
- Medications like Metformin are commonly prescribed.
- Diet management is crucial: reduce simple sugars, increase fiber, whole grains, and vegetables.
- Regular physical activity helps control blood sugar and weight.

Red flags:
- Frequent urination, extreme thirst, unexplained weight loss.
- Blurred vision or slow healing wounds.

Consult a healthcare professional for personalized guidance.
